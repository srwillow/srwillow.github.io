Histoires oubliées de mes Emard-dit-Potvin

Comme ils disent, il n'y a pas d'identité sans passé ni sans mémoire. Le but était de compléter une recherche plus approfondie du patronyme Emard-dit-Potvin et en ce, comprendre un peu plus l'histoire de mes aieux. Je me suis vite rendue compte que la portée de la recherche était trop large. La transmission de la mémoire de la famille est très importante... ce n'est qu'un début.

Welcome to my research into some Emard-dit-Potvin family genealogie & history. My goal was to answer some long standing questions that I and my parents had regarding our ancestors and through a website, disseminate the findings and updates to those family members who were interested in the stories. I am always happy to received any clarifications and/or additional information as well as new questions to be answered.


My fear was that there would be very little data but in today's modern digital world, it is in fact the opposite and would take a lifetime to gather, read, organize and disseminate. The material has been gathered primarily from primary sources, including Roman Catholic church registry entries, city directories and books. Other public domain information have been gathered from websites with well-defined sources. Source details can be found throughout the website. Essentially, I have tried to ensure that I have not captured some oft-repeated but incorrect data and speculations.
